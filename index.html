SisterWives on a Mission

We are Moms who understand how hard momming is in the best of times.
We are Moms who understand the importance of leaning on other Moms in the difficult times.
We are Moms connecting Moms so we all can have SisterWives when we need them.

Supports We Coordinate

Three friends who could always count on each other for anything - someone to watch the kids for few hours to get an assignment done, someone to drive the kids to scouts or practice, someone to grab toilet paper or laundry detergent when they see it during a pandemic - are what we call SisterWives. While every Mom needs these supports once in awhile, Moms who are also fighting cancer need them a whole lot more. They need more rest so their bodies can recover and recuperate. They need other Moms to talk to, vent to, and cry with who will understand all of it because they are also experiencing it. They need time to be the woman that they are separate from the mom, spouse, and whatever other hats they wear.
